Katrina Kaif's latest pics from the sets will make you eager about Bharat's trailer

Katrina Kaif took to her Instagram page to share pictures from the sets of Bharat. She stars alongside Salman Khan in the film.

The last two years have been amazing for Katrina Kaif. Her 2017 film Tiger Zinda Hai in which starred alongside Salman Khan was a blockbuster at the box office. The film was helmed by Ali Abbas Zafar. Last year, she was seen in Aanand L Rai's Zero which saw her reuniting with her Jab Tak Hai Jaan co-stars Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma.

In Zero, Kat played the role of an actress named Babita Kumari. The audience and the critics were in love with her character and called it one of her best performances so far. Further, the actress will be seen again in Ali Abbas Zafar's film titled Bharat. This will be their third film together. 

Bharat also stars Salman Khan in the lead role. The actors started the shooting of the film last year in August. Fans are excited again to see Salman and Kat together in Ali's film. To add more to their curiosity, Katrina Kaif sometimes shares pictures from the sets. We all know the Phatom actress will be seen in a saree-clad avatar in her next. However, this time her look for the film is not the ones we have seen her donning a saree.

Even today, Katrina Kaif shared two photos on her Insta story and as her post. She looks beautiful in both the pics and we are really excited to know about her character.



Last month, the makers shared the first teaser of Bharat which featured Salman Khan's five different looks. Now the audience is looking for to the trailer and to know about Katrina and other characters.

Bharat also stars Disha Patani and Sunil Grover in pivotal roles. The film will hit the screens on Eid this year.
