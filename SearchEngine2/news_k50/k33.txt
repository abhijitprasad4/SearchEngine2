Katrina Kaif opens up about her equation with Deepika Padukone

We all know that Katrina Kaif and Deepika Padukone did not gel well with each other due to their relationship with Ranbir Kapoor. The two have been making headlines for a long time. In a recent interview with Filmfare, Katrina spoke about how her equation with Deepika Padukone.

When the 'Zero' actress was quizzed about her equation with Deepika she revealed how their relationship has one where they don’t see each other eye to eye, and it is even now a relationship. Katrina said that when she got an invite and a message that said ‘you have to come and be a part of this' to get involved in the wedding reception of Ranveer Singh and Deepika she revealed how she found this sweet, paused for a moment and said that she felt like going.



She further added and said that how both of them came in the industry their teens, and now they aren’t teens anymore. She said how there is mutual respect and acknowledgment of each other and that it is nice that they have a sense of friendship now.



Meanwhile, Katrina will star in 'Bharat' next to Salman Khan while Deepika will be seen next in Meghna Gulzar's 'Chhapaak'.
